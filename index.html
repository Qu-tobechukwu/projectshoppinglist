Heart Behind It

A note on why Eden Creates exists — a gentle, handcrafted approach to storytelling in a noisy digital world.

International Inspiration: I draw from art, poetry, and gentle design — names like Morgan Harper Nichols inspire the tenderness I aim for. But I bring a distinct South African warmth: handwritten intimacy, tactile pages, and slow, prayerful craft.

What I make: flipbooks & poems, posters with short messages, curated collections, books that read like letters, podcasts that feel like sitting with a friend. I pair visual design with words so each piece comforts, encourages, and connects.

Why analog matters: Not everything should bend to instant digital consumption. Some stories need to be held, unfolded, and passed on. The Lord has placed a love for the tangible in this work — letters that take someone on a journey, books that keep their breath, art that ages like a memory.

“You make art prints, poems, and letters that speak life.”
